Curing the Thirsty

Vampirism certainly does not work out for everyone. More often than not, it is a curse and some could say only a fool would willingly seek it out. After another sleepless night, you have grown weary of this disease in your body that makes you thirst for the blood of others. Your Guild-Brothers, however - are always willing to help out. Lest they see you on their contract board, one day. To spare you of this fate - they have told you to seek out a witch, who holds the cure for your condition. This was a path previously taken by a member of their guild, Yip was his name. He, much like yourself, was a vampire haunted by their afflictions. You must follow in his footsteps to achieve a life beyond the terror that has overtaken your current existence. This task, however, is much easier said than done.
